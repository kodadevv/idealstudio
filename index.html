About Us

Ideal Design Studio is a full service real estate company located in Long Island, NY. We specialize in buying and selling solutions for real estate investors in our area. Our diverse team of real estate experts has experience with all types of buying and selling scenarios, making every transaction simple and stress free for everyone. We have all types of investment properties for sale and are confident that we can find something to meet the needs of every buyer. We are also well versed in most types of property sales and are certain that we can come up with a solution for every seller, whether it be a traditional or creative selling solution.
